Green Tea

Green Tea is a light, refreshing, and healthy drink made from unoxidized tea leaves. It is one of the most popular teas in the world, known for its clean taste, natural aroma, and numerous health benefits.
Green tea has a mild, slightly earthy flavor with a gentle bitterness that makes it unique and refreshing. Unlike stronger teas, it has a light and smooth taste that is easy to enjoy, especially when prepared correctly.
One of the most important features of green tea is its health value. It is rich in antioxidants and natural compounds that help support the body, improve focus, and boost overall wellness. Because of this, it is often included in healthy lifestyles and daily routines.
Green tea is also known for its calming effect. It can help you relax while still keeping your mind alert, making it a great choice for both morning and evening use.
This drink can be enjoyed hot or cold, depending on preference. When served hot, it provides a warm and soothing experience. When served cold, it becomes a refreshing and light beverage perfect for warm weather.

Green tea can also be customized by adding honey, lemon, or mint to improve the taste and create different flavors. It is simple to prepare and does not require many ingredients.
Whether used for relaxation or as part of a healthy routine, green tea is a clean, refreshing, and beneficial drink that offers both calmness and natural energy in every cup.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Green tea leaves or tea bag 🍵
  2. Hot water
  3. Honey (optional) 🍯
  4. Lemon (optional) 🍋
  5. Mint leaves (optional) 🌿

Steps to Make Green Tea👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water

  • Heat water until hot (not too boiling)

2. Add Tea

  • Place tea bag or leaves in cup

3. Pour Water

  • Pour hot water into the cup

4. Steep

  • Allow tea to soak

5. Remove Tea

  • Remove tea bag or strain leaves

6. Add Flavor (Optional)

  • Add honey, lemon, or mint

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Enjoy hot or cold

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t use boiling water (it can make tea bitter)
  • Steep for short time for better taste
  • Add honey for sweetness 🍯
  • Serve warm or chilled ❄️
