Masala Chai

Masala Chai is a warm, aromatic, and flavorful tea made by brewing black tea with a blend of spices, milk, and sugar. It is a traditional drink widely enjoyed for its rich taste, comforting warmth, and inviting aroma.
The word “masala” means a mixture of spices, and this tea is known for its unique combination of ingredients such as ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and sometimes black pepper. These spices give the tea a bold and slightly spicy flavor that makes it stand out from regular tea.
One of the most appealing things about Masala Chai is its deep and comforting taste. The black tea provides a strong base, while the milk adds creaminess and smoothness. The spices create a warm and fragrant experience that feels both relaxing and energizing at the same time.
This tea is especially popular during cold weather or in the mornings, as it helps warm the body and boost energy. It is also often enjoyed during social gatherings, where it brings people together with its rich aroma and inviting taste.
Masala Chai is not only delicious but also known for its health benefits. Ingredients like ginger and spices can help with digestion, improve circulation, and provide natural warmth to the body.
The tea is usually served hot and fresh, making it a perfect comfort drink. It can be adjusted based on personal preference by increasing or reducing the spices, sweetness, or milk.

Preparing Masala Chai involves boiling the ingredients together, which allows the flavors to fully blend and create a rich, full-bodied drink.
Whether enjoyed in the morning or during a relaxing break, Masala Chai is a bold, aromatic, and comforting tea that delivers warmth, flavor, and a rich cultural experience in every cup.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Black tea leaves or tea bag 🍵
  2. Milk 🥛
  3. Water
  4. Sugar 🍯
  5. Ginger 🫚
  6. Cardamom
  7. Cinnamon
  8. Cloves

Steps to Make Masala Chai👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water & Spices

  • Add water to a pot
  • Add ginger and spices
  • Boil to release flavor

2. Add Tea

  • Add black tea leaves or tea bag

3. Add Milk

  • Pour in milk

4. Simmer

  • Let it cook gently

5. Add Sugar

  • Add sugar and stir

6. Strain

  • Strain into a cup

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Enjoy hot

Tipes 💡

  • Adjust spices to your taste 🌿
  • Boil well for rich flavor
  • Use fresh ginger for best result 🫚
  • Serve hot for comfort
