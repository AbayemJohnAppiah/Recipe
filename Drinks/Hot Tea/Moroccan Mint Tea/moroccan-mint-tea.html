Moroccan Mint Tea

Moroccan Mint Tea is a fragrant, refreshing, and traditional drink made by combining green tea with fresh mint leaves and sugar. It is widely enjoyed for its bright aroma, smooth taste, and cooling effect, making it one of the most loved teas around the world.
This tea is known for its perfect balance of flavors. The green tea provides a light and slightly earthy base, while the fresh mint adds a cool, refreshing taste. Sugar is often added to create a gentle sweetness that balances the natural bitterness of the tea.
One of the most special things about Moroccan Mint Tea is its aroma. The smell of fresh mint mixed with warm tea creates a calming and inviting experience even before the first sip.
The drink is usually served hot, but it gives a cooling sensation because of the mint. This makes it suitable for both warm and cool weather. It is often enjoyed during social gatherings and is seen as a symbol of hospitality and welcome.
Moroccan Mint Tea is not just a drink but also a cultural experience. It is often prepared and served in a special way, sometimes poured from a height into small glasses to create a light foam on top.
The tea is also refreshing and soothing. It can help relax the body while keeping the mind alert, making it a perfect drink for both relaxation and refreshment.

It is simple to prepare at home with a few ingredients, and the level of sweetness can be adjusted based on preference.
Whether served to guests or enjoyed alone, Moroccan Mint Tea is a fresh, aromatic, and soothing drink that delivers a perfect blend of warmth, sweetness, and refreshing mint flavor in every cup.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Green tea 🍵
  2. Fresh mint leaves 🌿
  3. Sugar 🍯
  4. Hot water

Steps to Make Moroccan Mint Tea👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water

  • Heat water until hot

2. Add Tea

  • Put green tea into a teapot

3. Pour Water

  • Add hot water and let it steep briefly

4. Add Mint

  • Add fresh mint leaves

5. Add Sugar

  • Add sugar to taste

6. Mix & Steep

  • Allow flavors to blend

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Pour into cups (optional: from a height)

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh mint for best aroma 🌿
  • Don’t over-steep tea
  • Adjust sugar to taste 🍯
  • Serve hot for best experience
