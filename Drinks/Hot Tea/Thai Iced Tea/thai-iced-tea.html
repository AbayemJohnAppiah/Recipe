Thai Iced Tea

Thai Iced Tea is a rich, creamy, and refreshing drink made from strongly brewed tea mixed with sugar, spices, and milk, then served over ice. It is known for its bright orange color, smooth texture, and sweet, bold flavor that makes it stand out from regular iced teas.
Thai Iced Tea has a unique taste that combines strong tea flavor with creamy sweetness. The tea is usually brewed very strong, giving it a deep and bold base. This is then balanced with sweetened milk or evaporated milk, which adds a smooth and creamy texture.
One of the most striking features of Thai Iced Tea is its vibrant orange color. This makes it visually appealing and perfect for serving in clear glasses, especially for modern drink menus.
The drink is typically served cold with ice, making it very refreshing, especially in warm weather. The combination of cold temperature, creamy texture, and sweet flavor makes it both satisfying and enjoyable.
Thai Iced Tea is also often flavored with spices like star anise or vanilla, which give it a slightly unique and aromatic taste. These flavors make the drink more interesting and memorable.
It is commonly enjoyed as a street drink or dessert beverage and pairs well with spicy foods because its sweetness helps balance strong flavors.

Preparing Thai Iced Tea at home is simple and requires only a few steps. With the right balance of tea, milk, and sweetness, you can easily recreate this popular drink.
Whether enjoyed as a refreshing treat or a dessert drink, Thai Iced Tea is a bold, creamy, and sweet beverage that delivers a perfect mix of flavor, color, and refreshment in every glass.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Black tea or Thai tea mix 🍵
  2. Sugar 🍯
  3. Evaporated milk or condensed milk 🥛
  4. Water
  5. Ice cubes ❄️
  6. Vanilla or star anise (optional)

Steps to Make Thai Iced Tea👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water

  • Heat water until boiling

2. Brew Tea

  • Add tea leaves or tea mix
  • Let it brew strong

3. Strain Tea

  • Remove tea leaves

4. Add Sugar

  • Stir sugar while tea is hot

5. Cool Down ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Allow tea to cool

6. Prepare Glass

  • Fill glass with ice

7. Add Tea & Milk

  • Pour tea over ice
  • Add milk on top

8. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Stir and enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Brew tea strong for best flavor
  • Use condensed milk for sweetness 🥛
  • Serve very cold ❄️
  • Don’t skip the ice
