Bloody Mary

Bloody Mary is a classic and distinctive cocktail known for its rich, savory flavor and bold character. Unlike most cocktails that are sweet or fruity, Bloody Mary stands out because of its spicy, tangy, and slightly salty taste.
This drink is made using vodka, tomato juice, and a blend of spices and seasonings. The tomato juice gives it a thick and smooth texture, while the spices add depth and complexity to the flavor. Common seasonings include salt, pepper, hot sauce, and sometimes Worcestershire sauce, creating a strong and flavorful drink.
One of the most interesting things about Bloody Mary is its unique taste profile. It is not sweet like many other cocktails, but instead offers a savory experience that feels more like a light meal in a glass. This makes it very different and appealing to those who enjoy bold flavors.
Bloody Mary is often served with creative garnishes such as celery sticks, lemon slices, olives, or even more elaborate toppings. These garnishes not only improve the presentation but also add extra flavor and texture to the drink.
This cocktail is especially popular as a brunch drink and is often enjoyed in the morning or early afternoon. Many people believe it helps refresh and energize the body, making it a popular choice after long nights or during relaxed weekends.

Despite its complex flavor, Bloody Mary is quite simple to prepare at home. With the right balance of ingredients and seasoning, you can easily create a flavorful and satisfying drink.
Whether enjoyed during brunch or as a bold cocktail option, Bloody Mary is a rich, savory, and refreshing drink that delivers a unique combination of spice, tanginess, and smooth texture.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Vodka 🍸
  2. Tomato juice 🍅
  3. Lemon juice 🍋
  4. Salt and black pepper
  5. Hot sauce (optional) 🌶️
  6. Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  7. Ice cubes ❄️
  8. Celery or lemon slice (optional garnish)

Steps to Make Bloody Mary👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Glass

  • Fill a glass with ice cubes

2. Add Ingredients

  • Add vodka and tomato juice

3. Add Seasoning

  • Add lemon juice, salt, pepper, and hot sauce

4. Mix Well

  • Stir gently until well combined

5. Taste & Adjust

  • Adjust spice and seasoning

6. Garnish

  • Add celery stick or lemon slice

7. Enjoy ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Serve cold and enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Adjust spice to your taste 🌶️
  • Use fresh lemon juice 🍋
  • Serve very cold ❄️
  • Try different garnishes for style
