Green Goddess

Green Goddess is a fresh, light, and highly refreshing drink made from a blend of cucumber, celery, apple, and lemon. It is known for its bright green color, crisp taste, and clean, natural flavor that makes it a popular choice for healthy lifestyles.
This drink offers a unique balance of flavors. The cucumber provides a cool and refreshing base, the celery adds a slightly earthy and crisp taste, the apple brings natural sweetness, and the lemon adds a fresh citrus kick that brightens the entire drink. Together, they create a smooth and refreshing blend that feels light and energizing.
One of the best things about Green Goddess is its refreshing and cleansing feel. It is often enjoyed as a detox-style drink because it is light, hydrating, and packed with natural ingredients that help refresh the body.
The texture is usually smooth and light, making it easy to drink at any time of the day. It is especially popular in the morning or as a healthy midday refreshment.
This drink is also rich in vitamins and nutrients. The combination of fresh vegetables and fruits provides natural energy and supports overall wellness. Because of this, it is often chosen by people looking for a healthy and natural drink option.

Green Goddess is simple to prepare at home with a blender or juicer and a few fresh ingredients. It can also be customized by adding ginger for a spicy kick or mint leaves for extra freshness.
Whether enjoyed as part of a healthy routine or simply as a refreshing drink, Green Goddess is a light, clean, and nutritious beverage that delivers freshness, hydration, and natural flavor in every sip.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Cucumber 🥒
  2. Celery
  3. Apple 🍏
  4. Lemon 🍋
  5. Water
  6. Ice cubes ❄️
  7. Ginger or mint (optional)

Steps to Make Green Goddess👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Ingredients

  • Wash cucumber, celery, and apple
  • Cut into small pieces
  • Peel lemon and remove seeds

2. Add Ingredients

  • Add all ingredients into blender
  • Add a little water

3. Add Ice

  • Add ice cubes

4. Blend

  • Blend until smooth

5. Strain (Optional)

  • Strain for a lighter juice texture

6. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh ingredients for best taste 🥒
  • Add apple for natural sweetness 🍏
  • Add ginger for extra kick 🌶️
  • Serve very cold ❄️
