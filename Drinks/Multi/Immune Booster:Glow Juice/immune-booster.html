Immune Booster / Glow Juice

Immune Booster Juice (also known as Glow Juice) is a bright, refreshing, and nutrient-rich drink made from a powerful combination of carrot, orange, and ginger. It is known for its vibrant orange color, fresh citrus flavor, and natural energy boost that makes it a popular choice for healthy living.
This drink delivers a well-balanced flavor. The carrot provides a mild natural sweetness and smooth base, the orange adds a fresh and tangy citrus taste, and the ginger brings a light spicy kick that makes the drink more exciting and refreshing. Together, they create a flavorful and energizing blend.
One of the best things about Immune Booster Juice is its health benefits. It is rich in vitamins, especially vitamin C from oranges and vitamin A from carrots, which help support the immune system and improve overall body wellness. Ginger also helps with digestion and adds a natural warming effect.
The juice has a smooth and slightly thick texture, making it satisfying to drink while still feeling light and refreshing. It is perfect for starting the day, boosting energy, or refreshing the body after a long day.
This drink is often called “Glow Juice” because of its bright color and the belief that it helps improve skin health and overall appearance. It gives a fresh and healthy feel with every sip.

It is easy to prepare at home with a blender or juicer and a few simple ingredients. You can also adjust the taste by adding honey for extra sweetness or lemon for more tanginess.
Whether enjoyed in the morning or as a healthy refreshment, Immune Booster / Glow Juice is a vibrant, nutritious, and refreshing drink that delivers natural energy, freshness, and wellness in every glass.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Carrots 🥕
  2. Oranges 🍊
  3. Fresh ginger 🫚
  4. Water
  5. Honey (optional) 🍯
  6. Ice cubes ❄️
  7. Lemon juice (optional) 🍋

Steps to Make Immune Booster / Glow Juice👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Ingredients

  • Wash carrots and peel them
  • Peel oranges
  • Peel and slice ginger

2. Cut Ingredients

  • Cut carrots into small pieces
  • Separate orange segments

3. Blend

  • Add carrots, oranges, and ginger into blender
  • Add a little water
  • Blend until smooth

4. Strain (Optional)

  • Strain to remove pulp if needed

5. Taste & Adjust

  • Add honey or lemon juice if desired

6. Chill ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Refrigerate or add ice cubes

7. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh ginger for best flavor 🫚
  • Adjust ginger for spice level
  • Use ripe oranges for sweetness 🍊
  • Serve cold for best experience ❄️
