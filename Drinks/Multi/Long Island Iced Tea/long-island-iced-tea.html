Long Island Iced Tea

Long Island Iced Tea is a famous and powerful cocktail known for its unique combination of multiple spirits and its smooth, refreshing taste. Despite its name, it does not actually contain tea. Instead, it gets its “iced tea” appearance from the blend of ingredients and the addition of cola, which gives it a similar color.
This cocktail is made using a mix of vodka, rum, gin, tequila, and triple sec, combined with sour mix and a splash of cola. The result is a well-balanced drink that blends different flavors into one smooth and refreshing experience.
One of the most interesting things about Long Island Iced Tea is how it tastes surprisingly smooth despite containing several types of alcohol. The citrusy sour mix and cola help balance the strong spirits, making it easier to drink than expected.
The flavor is a mix of sweet, tangy, and slightly bold notes, creating a refreshing and lively drink. It is often served with ice and garnished with a slice of lemon, which adds extra freshness.
This cocktail is very popular at parties, bars, and social gatherings because of its strong and energizing effect. It is often chosen by people who want a drink that is both flavorful and powerful.

Even though it looks and tastes smooth, it is important to know that Long Island Iced Tea is quite strong due to the combination of different spirits. It is best enjoyed responsibly and slowly.
Whether served at a party or enjoyed during a relaxed evening, Long Island Iced Tea is a bold, refreshing, and well-balanced cocktail that delivers a unique mix of flavors in every sip.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Vodka 🍸
  2. Rum
  3. Gin
  4. Tequila
  5. Triple Sec
  6. Sour mix 🍋
  7. Cola 🥤
  8. Ice cubes ❄️
  9. Lemon slice (optional)

Steps to Make Long Island Iced Tea👩‍🍳

1. Fill Glass with Ice

  • Add ice cubes into a tall glass

2. Add Spirits

  • Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, and triple sec

3. Add Sour Mix

  • Add sour mix for citrus flavor

4. Top with Cola

  • Add a splash of cola

5. Stir Gently

  • Mix all ingredients together

6. Garnish

  • Add lemon slice

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Serve cold and enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t add too much cola
  • Use fresh sour mix for best taste 🍋
  • Serve very cold ❄️
  • Drink responsibly
