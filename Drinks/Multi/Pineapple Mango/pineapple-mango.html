Pineapple Mango

Pineapple Mango is a sweet, smooth, and refreshing drink made by combining ripe pineapple and juicy mango. This tropical blend is often called a “sunshine drink” because of its bright yellow color and vibrant, fruity flavor that instantly feels warm and uplifting.
The taste of pineapple mango is perfectly balanced. The mango brings a rich, creamy sweetness, while the pineapple adds a slightly tangy and refreshing kick. Together, they create a smooth and flavorful drink that is both sweet and refreshing at the same time.
One of the best things about this drink is its tropical character. Every sip feels fresh, juicy, and full of natural fruit flavor, making it ideal for hot weather or when you want something light and energizing.
The texture can be adjusted depending on how you prepare it. It can be made as a light juice or a thicker smoothie-style drink. Adding ice makes it even more refreshing, giving it a cool and satisfying finish.
This drink is also very nutritious. Both pineapple and mango are rich in vitamins and natural nutrients, making the drink not only delicious but also beneficial for the body.
Pineapple mango is easy to prepare at home with just a few ingredients and a blender. It can also be customized by adding coconut milk for a creamy tropical version or a splash of lime for extra freshness.
Whether served as a refreshing drink, a smoothie, or a party beverage, pineapple mango is a sweet, tropical, and energizing blend that delivers a burst of sunshine in every sip.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Ripe pineapple 🍍
  2. Ripe mango 🥭
  3. Water or coconut milk 🥥
  4. Sugar or honey (optional) 🍯
  5. Ice cubes ❄️
  6. Lime juice (optional) 🍋

Steps to Make Pineapple Mango👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Fruits

  • Peel pineapple and remove core
  • Peel mango and remove seed
  • Cut both into small pieces

2. Add Ingredients

  • Add pineapple and mango into blender
  • Add water or coconut milk

3. Add Ice

  • Add ice cubes

4. Blend

  • Blend until smooth and creamy

5. Adjust Taste

  • Add sugar, honey, or lime juice if needed

6. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use ripe fruits for natural sweetness 🍍🥭
  • Add coconut milk for a creamy tropical taste 🥥
  • Serve very cold for best experience ❄️
  • Don’t add too much water
