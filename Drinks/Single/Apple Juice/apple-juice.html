Apple Juice

Apple Juice is a naturally sweet and refreshing drink made from fresh apples. It is one of the most popular fruit juices in the world because of its smooth taste, light texture, and natural sweetness.
Apple juice is loved for its clean and fresh flavor. It has a gentle sweetness that is not too strong, making it enjoyable for both children and adults. When made fresh, it has a bright taste that feels refreshing, especially on a hot day.
One of the best things about apple juice is its simplicity. It is made using just apples and sometimes a little water or sweetener, depending on preference. Despite its simple ingredients, it delivers a rich and satisfying flavor.
Fresh apple juice also contains important nutrients and natural sugars that provide energy. It is often seen as a healthier alternative to sugary drinks because it comes directly from fruit.
Apple juice can be served chilled for a refreshing experience or at room temperature. It is commonly enjoyed at breakfast, as a snack, or alongside meals.

It can also be combined with other fruits like oranges or carrots to create mixed juices with different flavors.
Whether served fresh at home or bought from stores, apple juice is a smooth, refreshing, and naturally sweet drink that is perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Fresh apples 🍎
  2. Water
  3. Sugar (optional)
  4. Lemon juice (optional) 🍋

Steps to Make Apple Juice👩‍🍳

1. Wash Apples

  • Rinse apples properly

2. Cut Apples

  • Cut into small pieces
  • Remove seeds

3. Blend or Juice

  • Blend apples with a little water
  • OR use a juicer

4. Strain Juice

  • Use a sieve or cloth
  • Remove pulp

5. Add Flavor

  • Add sugar or lemon juice if needed

6. Chill (Optional) ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Refrigerate for better taste

7. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use sweet apples for better taste 🍎
  • Don’t add too much water
  • Chill before serving ❄️
  • Add lemon for freshness 🍋
