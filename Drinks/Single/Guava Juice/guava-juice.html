Guava Juice

Guava Juice is a sweet, slightly tangy, and refreshing drink made from ripe guavas. It is known for its unique tropical flavor, soft pink or light green color, and smooth fruity taste.
Guava juice has a strong natural aroma and a balanced taste that combines sweetness with a mild tanginess. This makes it different from many other fruit juices, giving it a rich and refreshing character that many people enjoy.
One of the best things about guava juice is its natural sweetness, which often reduces the need for added sugar. When blended well, it produces a smooth and slightly thick juice that feels very satisfying to drink.
Guava juice is also highly nutritious. Guavas are rich in vitamins and natural nutrients that support health and provide energy. Because of this, guava juice is often enjoyed as both a tasty and healthy drink.
This juice can be served on its own or combined with other fruits like apple, pineapple, or orange to create exciting mixed flavors. It is best enjoyed chilled, especially on hot days when a refreshing drink is needed.

Guava juice is also very easy to prepare at home with just a few ingredients. With a blender or juicer, you can quickly turn fresh guavas into a delicious drink.
Whether served as a daily refreshment or a special tropical drink, guava juice is a flavorful, nutritious, and refreshing beverage that delivers a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Ripe guavas 🍈
  2. Water
  3. Sugar or honey (optional)
  4. Lemon juice (optional) 🍋

Steps to Make Guava Juice👩‍🍳

1. Wash Guavas

  • Rinse guavas thoroughly

2. Cut Guavas

  • Slice into small pieces
  • Remove seeds if needed

3. Blend

  • Add guavas to blender
  • Add water
  • Blend until smooth

4. Strain Juice

  • Use a sieve to remove seeds and pulp

5. Add Flavor

  • Add sugar or lemon juice if needed

6. Chill ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Refrigerate for better taste

7. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use ripe guavas for sweetness 🍈
  • Strain well for smooth texture
  • Add lemon for extra freshness 🍋
  • Serve cold for best experience ❄️
