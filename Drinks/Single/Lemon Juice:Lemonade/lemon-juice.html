Lemon Juice

Lemon Juice is a refreshing and widely enjoyed drink made from fresh lemons. It is known for its sharp, tangy flavor balanced with sweetness, creating a perfect combination of sour and sweet in every sip.
Lemon juice, also called lemonade when sweetened and diluted, is one of the simplest and most popular homemade drinks in the world. It is made using fresh lemon juice mixed with water and sweetener, making it light, refreshing, and very easy to prepare.
One of the best things about lemonade is its refreshing taste. The natural sourness of lemons wakes up the senses, while sugar or honey balances it to create a smooth and enjoyable drink. This makes it perfect for hot weather or when you need something cooling and energizing.
Lemon juice is also known for its health benefits. Lemons contain vitamin C and other nutrients that help support the immune system and keep the body refreshed. Because of this, it is often considered both a tasty and healthy drink option.
Lemonade can also be customized in many ways. Some people add mint leaves, ginger, or even fruits like strawberries to create different flavors. It is usually served cold with ice cubes to make it even more refreshing.

This drink is very common in homes, restaurants, and street food stalls because it is quick to prepare and very satisfying. It is also a great drink to serve at gatherings, parties, or during hot afternoons.
Whether served plain or with added flavors, lemon juice (lemonade) is a simple, refreshing, and energizing drink that delivers the perfect balance of sourness and sweetness.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Fresh lemons 🍋
  2. Water
  3. Sugar or honey 🍯
  4. Ice cubes ❄️
  5. Mint leaves (optional) 🌿
  6. Ginger (optional)

Steps to Make Lemon Juice👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Lemons

  • Wash lemons properly
  • Cut and squeeze juice

2. Mix Juice

  • Add lemon juice into a jug
  • Add water

3. Add Sweetener

  • Add sugar or honey
  • Stir well

4. Add Extra Flavor (Optional)

  • Add mint leaves or ginger if desired

5. Chill ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Add ice cubes or refrigerate

6. Serve

  • Pour into glass
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh lemons for best taste 🍋
  • Adjust sugar based on preference
  • Add mint for extra freshness 🌿
  • Serve very cold for best experience ❄️
