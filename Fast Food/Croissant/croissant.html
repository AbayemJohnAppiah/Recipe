Croissant

Croissant is a famous buttery pastry that originated from France and is loved all around the world. It is known for its light, flaky layers and rich buttery flavor, making it one of the most popular bakery items.
A croissant is made from layered dough that is folded multiple times with butter inside. This process creates thin layers that puff up when baked, giving it a soft inside and a crispy golden outside. The shape is usually crescent-like, which is where the name “croissant” comes from.
Croissants can be enjoyed plain or filled with ingredients like chocolate, cheese, jam, or even ham. They are commonly eaten for breakfast or as a snack and are often served with coffee or tea.
One of the most special things about croissants is their texture. When freshly baked, they are warm, soft, and melt in the mouth, with a rich buttery taste that makes them very satisfying.

They are found in bakeries, cafes, and hotels around the world because of their elegant look and delicious taste. Although they look simple, making croissants requires patience and careful layering of dough and butter.
Whether plain or filled, croissants are a soft, flaky, and luxurious pastry enjoyed by people everywhere.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour
  2. Butter 🧈
  3. Milk 🥛
  4. Sugar
  5. Yeast
  6. Salt
  7. Eggs 🥚

Optional Fillings:

  1. Chocolate 🍫
  2. Jam 🍓
  3. Cheese 🧀

Steps to Make Croissant👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Dough

  • Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, milk, and eggs
  • Knead until smooth

2. Let Dough Rest ⏱ (1–2 hours)

  • Cover dough
  • Allow it to rise

3. Prepare Butter Layer

  • Flatten butter into a thin sheet

4. Layer Dough & Butter

  • Roll dough and place butter inside
  • Fold carefully

5. Fold Multiple Times

  • Roll and fold dough 3–4 times
  • This creates flaky layers

6. Shape Croissants

  • Cut dough into triangles
  • Roll into crescent shape

7. Final Rise ⏱ (45–60 minutes)

  • Let shaped croissants rise

8. Bake ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Bake until golden brown

9. Serve

  • Serve warm
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Keep butter cold for flaky layers 🧈
  • Don’t rush folding process
  • Let dough rise properly
  • Bake until golden for best texture 🔥
