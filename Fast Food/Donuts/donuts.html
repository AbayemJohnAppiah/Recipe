Donuts

Donut (also spelled doughnut) is a popular sweet treat enjoyed all over the world. It is made from soft dough that is shaped, fried until golden, and often coated with sugar, glaze, or chocolate.
Donuts are known for their soft and fluffy inside with a slightly crispy outside. They usually have a round shape with a hole in the middle, although some are filled with cream, chocolate, or jam instead.
The taste of donuts is sweet and comforting, making them perfect for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. They can be served plain, dusted with sugar, glazed with icing, or decorated with toppings like sprinkles.
One of the best things about donuts is their variety. You can make different flavors such as chocolate, vanilla, or even fruit-flavored donuts. This makes them enjoyable for both kids and adults.
Donuts are often paired with drinks like coffee, tea, or milk, making them a complete and satisfying treat. They are commonly sold in bakeries, cafes, and street food spots because of their popularity.

Making donuts at home is also a fun experience. It allows you to control the ingredients and create fresh, soft donuts with your preferred toppings.
Whether simple or decorated, donuts remain a soft, sweet, and delicious treat that brings joy to people everywhere.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour
  2. Yeast
  3. Sugar
  4. Milk 🥛
  5. Eggs 🥚
  6. Butter or oil
  7. Salt
  8. Oil (for frying)

For the Toppings:

  1. Sugar
  2. Chocolate 🍫
  3. Glaze
  4. Sprinkles

Steps to Make Donuts👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Dough

  • Mix flour, yeast, sugar, milk, eggs, and butter
  • Knead until smooth

2. Let Dough Rise ⏱ (45–60 minutes)

  • Cover and allow to rise
  • Dough should double in size

3. Shape the Donuts

  • Roll out dough
  • Cut into donut shapes

4. Second Rise ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Leave shaped donuts to rise again

5. Heat Oil

  • Heat oil in a pan

6. Fry the Donuts ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Fry until golden brown
  • Turn for even cooking

7. Drain Oil

  • Remove and place on paper

8. Add Toppings

  • Add sugar, glaze, or chocolate

9. Serve

  • Serve warm
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Let dough rise properly for softness
  • Fry on medium heat 🔥
  • Don’t overcrowd the pan
  • Add toppings while warm
