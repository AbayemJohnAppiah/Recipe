French Fries

French Fries are one of the most popular and widely enjoyed snacks and side dishes in the world. They are made from potatoes that are cut into thin strips and deep-fried until they become golden, crispy on the outside, and soft on the inside.
French fries are loved for their simple but addictive taste. The crispy texture combined with a soft and fluffy center makes them very satisfying to eat. They are usually lightly salted, but can also be flavored with spices, cheese, or sauces.
Fries are commonly served as a side dish with burgers, fried chicken, hot dogs, and sandwiches. They are also enjoyed on their own as a quick snack, especially with ketchup, mayonnaise, cheese sauce, or spicy dips.
One of the best things about French fries is how easy they are to prepare and how well they pair with almost any meal. They are found in fast-food restaurants, street food stalls, and homes around the world.

Fries are especially popular among young people because of their crispy texture and delicious taste. They can also be baked or air-fried for a healthier version while still keeping their flavor.
Whether served as a side dish or snack, French fries remain a crispy, tasty, and globally loved food that fits perfectly into any meal.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Potatoes 🥔
  2. Salt
  3. Oil (for frying)
  4. Pepper (optional)
  5. Paprika or spices (optional)

Steps to Make French Fries👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Potatoes

  • Peel potatoes
  • Cut into thin strips

2. Soak Potatoes ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Place in cold water
  • Remove excess starch

3. Dry Potatoes ⏱ (5–10 minutes)

  • Pat dry with a clean cloth

4. Heat Oil

  • Heat oil in a deep pan

5. First Fry ⏱ (8–10 minutes)

  • Fry potatoes until soft but not brown
  • Remove and rest

6. Second Fry ⏱ (5–7 minutes)

  • Fry again until golden and crispy

7. Season

  • Add salt and spices

8. Serve

  • Serve hot
  • Add ketchup or sauce

Tipes 💡

  • Soaking helps fries become crispy 🥔
  • Fry twice for best texture 🔥
  • Don’t overcrowd the pan
  • Serve immediately for crunch
