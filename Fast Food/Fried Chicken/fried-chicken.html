Fried Chicken

Fried Chicken is one of the most popular and widely enjoyed meals around the world. It is made by seasoning chicken pieces, coating them in flour or batter, and frying them until they become golden brown, crispy on the outside, and juicy on the inside.
Fried chicken is loved for its perfect balance of texture and flavor. The outer layer is crunchy and well-seasoned, while the inside remains tender and full of flavor. This contrast makes every bite satisfying and enjoyable.
The seasoning is what makes fried chicken special. A mix of spices such as salt, pepper, garlic, and other flavors is used to give the chicken a rich and delicious taste. Some recipes also marinate the chicken before frying to make it even more flavorful.
Fried chicken can be eaten in many ways. It is often served with fries, rice, or salad, and can also be enjoyed on its own as a snack. It is a common meal at parties, restaurants, and fast-food spots because it is easy to share and loved by many people.
One of the best things about fried chicken is its versatility. You can make it mild or spicy, crispy or soft, depending on your preference. It can also be prepared at home using simple ingredients while still achieving a delicious result.

Although it is considered a comfort or fast food, homemade fried chicken can be made healthier by controlling the oil and ingredients used.
Whether served as a main meal or a snack, fried chicken remains a crispy, juicy, and satisfying dish that continues to be loved worldwide.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Chicken pieces 🍗
  2. Flour
  3. Salt
  4. Pepper
  5. Garlic powder
  6. Seasoning cubes
  7. Water or milk
  8. Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps to Make Fried Chicken👩‍🍳

1. Clean the Chicken

  • Wash chicken pieces properly
  • Drain water

2. Season the Chicken

  • Add salt, pepper, garlic, and seasoning
  • Mix well

3. Marinate ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Allow chicken to rest
  • This helps absorb flavor

4. Prepare Coating

  • Put flour in a bowl
  • Add a little seasoning

5. Coat the Chicken

  • Dip chicken in flour
  • Ensure it is fully covered

6. Heat Oil

  • Pour oil into a pan
  • Heat until ready

7. Fry the Chicken ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Add chicken to hot oil
  • Fry until golden brown
  • Turn occasionally

8. Drain Oil

  • Remove chicken
  • Place on paper to remove excess oil

9. Serve

  • Serve hot
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overcrowd the pan
  • Fry on medium heat for even cooking 🔥
  • Marinate for better flavor
  • Serve hot for crispy texture
