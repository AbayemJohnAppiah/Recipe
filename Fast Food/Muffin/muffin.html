Muffin

Muffin is a soft, sweet baked snack that is enjoyed around the world as a breakfast item or dessert. It is similar to a small cake but usually less sweet and baked in a round cup shape, making it easy to hold and eat.
Muffins are known for their soft, fluffy texture and rich flavor. They are made from a simple batter that includes flour, sugar, eggs, milk, and butter or oil. Once baked, they rise into soft, golden treats with a slightly crispy top and a tender inside.
One of the best things about muffins is their variety. They can be made in many flavors such as chocolate, vanilla, blueberry, banana, or even savory versions with cheese and vegetables. This makes them suitable for different tastes and occasions.
Muffins are commonly eaten for breakfast, snacks, or as a light dessert. They are popular in bakeries, cafes, and homes because they are quick to prepare and easy to carry.

They are also a great option for lunchboxes and quick snacks because they stay fresh for a long time and do not require extra toppings or preparation before eating. Whether plain or filled with fruits and chocolate chips, muffins are soft, warm, and delicious baked treats that bring comfort and sweetness to any moment.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour
  2. Sugar
  3. Baking powder
  4. Salt
  5. Milk 🥛
  6. Eggs 🥚
  7. Butter or oil
  8. Vanilla extract

Optional Add-ons:

  1. Chocolate chips 🍫
  2. Blueberries 🫐
  3. Banana 🍌

Steps to Make Muffin👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Batter

  • Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  • Add milk, eggs, and butter
  • Stir until smooth

2. Add Flavors

  • Add vanilla or chocolate chips/fruits

3. Prepare Muffin Tray

  • Grease or line muffin cups

4. Fill Cups

  • Pour batter into cups (do not overfill)

5. Bake ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Bake in preheated oven
  • Until golden and risen

6. Cool Down

  • Remove from oven
  • Let cool slightly

7. Serve

  • Serve warm or cold
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overmix batter (keeps muffins fluffy)
  • Fill cups only ¾ full
  • Use fresh ingredients for better taste
  • Let them cool before eating 🔥
