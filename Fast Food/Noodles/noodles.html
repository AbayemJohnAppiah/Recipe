Noodles

Noodles are a popular and versatile food enjoyed in many countries around the world. They are made from wheat flour dough that is stretched or cut into thin strands, then cooked by boiling or frying with sauces, vegetables, and proteins.
Noodles are loved for their soft texture and ability to absorb flavor. When cooked properly, they become tender yet slightly chewy, making them very satisfying to eat. They can be prepared in many styles, including fried noodles, soup noodles, or spicy stir-fried versions.
One of the best things about noodles is how flexible they are. You can add chicken, beef, eggs, or vegetables depending on your preference. Different sauces like soy sauce, chili sauce, or curry sauce can completely change the taste of the dish.
Noodles are commonly eaten as a quick meal, lunch, or dinner. They are popular in street food stalls, restaurants, and homes because they are fast to prepare and very filling.

In many places, noodles are also considered comfort food because they are warm, flavorful, and easy to customize. They are especially enjoyed when spicy or served with rich sauces and toppings.
Whether simple or fully loaded, noodles are a delicious, fast, and satisfying dish enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Noodles (instant or fresh) 🍜
  2. Water
  3. Vegetable oil
  4. Onion
  5. Carrots 🥕
  6. Cabbage
  7. Eggs 🥚
  8. Chicken or beef 🍗
  9. Soy sauce or seasoning
  10. Pepper 🌶️

Steps to Make Noodles👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water

  • Heat water in a pot
  • Bring to boil

2. Cook Noodles ⏱ (4–8 minutes)

  • Add noodles to boiling water
  • Cook until soft
  • Drain water

3. Prepare Ingredients

  • Slice vegetables and meat
  • Beat eggs

4. Fry Ingredients

  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Fry onions, meat, and vegetables
  • Add eggs and scramble

5. Add Noodles ⏱ (5–10 minutes)

  • Add cooked noodles into pan
  • Mix well

6. Add Sauce & Seasoning

  • Add soy sauce and spices
  • Stir evenly

7. Serve

  • Serve hot
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overcook noodles
  • Stir gently to avoid breaking them
  • Add sauces gradually for best flavor
  • Serve hot for best taste 🔥
