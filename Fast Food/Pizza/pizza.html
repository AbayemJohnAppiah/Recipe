Pizza

Ingredients 🥕

For the Dough:

  1. Flour
  2. Yeast
  3. Water
  4. Sugar
  5. Salt
  6. Olive oil

For the Toppings:

  1. Tomato sauce 🍅
  2. Cheese 🧀
  3. Pepperoni or meat 🍖
  4. Vegetables (onion,
    pepper, etc.) 🥗

Steps to Make Pizza👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Dough

  • Mix flour, yeast,
    sugar, salt, and water
  • Knead until smooth

2. Let Dough Rise ⏱ (45–60 minutes)

  • Cover and allow to rise
  • Dough should double
    in size

3. Shape the Dough

  • Roll into a flat round shape

4. Add Sauce

  • Spread tomato sauce evenly

5. Add Toppings

  • Add cheese, meat, and
    vegetables

6. Bake the Pizza ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Bake in oven at high
    temperature
  • Until cheese melts and
    crust is golden

7. Serve

  • Slice and serve hot
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use high heat for
    crispy crust 🔥
  • Don’t overload with
    toppings
  • Let dough rise properly
  • Use good cheese for best
    taste 🧀
