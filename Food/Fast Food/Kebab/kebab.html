Kebab

Kebab is a flavorful and widely enjoyed street food found in many parts of the world, including Ghana. It consists of pieces of meat-such as beef, chicken, or goat-seasoned with spices, placed on sticks (skewers), and grilled over fire until smoky, juicy, and delicious.
Kebab is known for its rich taste and attractive appearance. The meat is often marinated with spices, giving it a deep flavor before it is grilled. As it cooks over fire, it develops a slightly crispy outside while remaining tender and juicy inside.
In Ghana, kebab is a very popular street food, especially in the evenings. It is commonly sold at roadside spots where the smell of grilled meat fills the air, attracting many people. It is often served hot and sometimes paired with sliced onions, pepper, or bread.
One of the best things about kebab is its simplicity. With just a few ingredients and proper grilling, you can create a delicious and satisfying meal or snack. It is also very convenient to eat, making it perfect for people on the go.

Kebab can be enjoyed as a snack, side dish, or even part of a full meal. Its smoky flavor and juicy texture make it a favorite for many meat lovers.
Whether bought from a street vendor or made at home, kebab remains a tasty, smoky, and satisfying dish that brings people together through its rich flavor.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Beef, chicken, or goat meat 🍖
  2. Onion
  3. Pepper 🌶️
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Salt
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Skewers (sticks)

Steps to Make Kebab👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Meat

  • Cut meat into small pieces

2. Season the Meat

  • Add salt, pepper, garlic, ginger, and seasoning
  • Mix well

3. Marinate

  • Leave meat to absorb flavors

4. Prepare Skewers

  • Insert meat onto sticks
  • Add onion slices if desired

5. Heat Grill or Fire

  • Prepare charcoal or grill

6. Grill the Kebab ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Place skewers on fire
  • Turn regularly
  • Cook until browned and done

7. Serve

  • Serve hot
  • Add onions or pepper

Tipes 💡

  • Marinate well for best flavor
  • Turn regularly to avoid burning 🔥
  • Use moderate heat
  • Serve hot for best taste
