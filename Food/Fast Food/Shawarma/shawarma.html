Shawarma

Shawarma is a delicious and widely loved street food that originated from the Middle East, especially countries like Lebanon and Turkey. It has become very popular around the world, including in Ghana, where it is enjoyed as a quick and tasty meal.
Shawarma is made by wrapping thin slices of seasoned meat-such as chicken or beef-inside a soft flatbread or pita, along with vegetables and creamy sauces. The meat is usually marinated with spices and cooked until juicy and flavorful, giving shawarma its rich and unique taste.
What makes shawarma special is its combination of flavors and textures. The soft bread, juicy meat, crunchy vegetables, and creamy sauces all come together to create a balanced and satisfying meal.
In Ghana, shawarma has become a favorite fast food, often sold at roadside spots and food joints, especially in the evenings. It is loved because it is convenient, affordable, and very filling.

Shawarma is also highly customizable. You can choose your preferred type of meat, adjust the level of spice, and add ingredients like cabbage, carrots, onions, and sausage. Different sauces such as mayonnaise, ketchup, and pepper sauce can also be added for extra flavor.
Whether enjoyed as a quick snack or a full meal, shawarma is a flavorful, convenient, and satisfying dish that continues to grow in popularity worldwide.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Shawarma bread (flatbread or pita) 🫓
  2. Chicken or beef 🍗🥩
  3. Cabbage
  4. Carrots 🥕
  5. Onion
  6. Sausage (optional)
  7. Mayonnaise
  8. Ketchup
  9. Pepper sauce 🌶️

Steps to Make Shawarma👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Meat

  • Season chicken or beef with spices
  • Cook or grill until done
  • Slice into small pieces

2. Prepare Vegetables

  • Slice cabbage, carrots, and onions

3. Warm the Bread

  • Heat the shawarma bread slightly
  • Make it soft

4. Add Fillings

  • Place meat on the bread
  • Add vegetables and sausage

5. Add Sauce

  • Add mayonnaise, ketchup, and pepper sauce

6. Wrap the Shawarma

  • Fold and roll tightly

7. Toast (Optional)

  • Grill lightly for crispy texture

8. Serve

  • Serve warm
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overfill the wrap
  • Use fresh vegetables 🥗
  • Marinate meat for better taste
  • Toast lightly for crispy texture 🔥
