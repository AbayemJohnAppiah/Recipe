Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad is a popular and classic salad known for its rich, creamy flavor and crunchy texture. It is made mainly with fresh romaine lettuce, crispy croutons, grated cheese, and a special Caesar dressing that gives the salad its unique taste.
Caesar salad is loved for its perfect balance of textures. The lettuce is fresh and crunchy, the croutons add crispiness, and the dressing brings a creamy and slightly tangy flavor. The addition of cheese, usually parmesan, gives it a rich and savory taste.
One of the key features of Caesar salad is its dressing. It is made using ingredients like oil, garlic, lemon juice, and other seasonings, creating a bold and flavorful sauce that coats the lettuce evenly. This dressing is what makes Caesar salad stand out from other salads.
Caesar salad can be served as a side dish or turned into a full meal by adding grilled chicken, shrimp, or other proteins. This makes it both flexible and satisfying for different occasions.

It is commonly found in restaurants and is also easy to prepare at home with simple ingredients. Because of its creamy dressing and crunchy elements, it feels more filling than many other salads.
Whether served plain or with added protein, Caesar salad is a rich, crunchy, and delicious dish that continues to be a favorite around the world.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Romaine lettuce 🥬
  2. Croutons
  3. Parmesan cheese 🧀
  4. Olive oil
  5. Garlic
  6. Lemon juice 🍋
  7. Salt
  8. Pepper

Optional:

  1. Grilled chicken 🍗
  2. Eggs 🥚

Steps to Make Caesar Salad👩‍🍳

1. Prepare the Lettuce

  • Wash and dry lettuce
  • Tear into pieces

2. Prepare the Dressing

  • Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper
  • Stir well

3. Combine Lettuce & Dressing

  • Pour dressing over lettuce
  • Toss gently

4. Add Croutons

  • Sprinkle croutons on top

5. Add Cheese

  • Add grated parmesan cheese

6. Add Protein (Optional)

  • Add grilled chicken or eggs if desired

7. Serve

  • Serve fresh
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh lettuce for crunch 🥬
  • Don’t add too much dressing
  • Add croutons last to keep them crispy
  • Serve immediately
