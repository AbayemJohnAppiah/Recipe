Ghanaian Salad

Ghanaian Salad is a popular and refreshing dish enjoyed across Ghana. It is commonly served at parties, restaurants, and family gatherings, and is loved for its colorful appearance and rich, creamy taste.
This salad is made using a mix of fresh vegetables such as lettuce, cabbage, tomatoes, cucumbers, and carrots. It is often combined with other ingredients like boiled eggs, baked beans, and sometimes pasta or potatoes, making it more filling and satisfying.
One of the special things about Ghanaian salad is its balance of textures and flavors. The vegetables provide a fresh and crunchy bite, while ingredients like eggs and beans add softness and richness. The combination creates a salad that is both refreshing and hearty.
The dressing plays an important role in this dish. It is usually made with mayonnaise, salad cream, or a mix of both, giving the salad a smooth and creamy taste. This makes it different from many international salads that use lighter dressings.
Ghanaian salad is often served as a side dish alongside meals like fried rice, jollof rice, grilled chicken, or fried fish. It adds freshness to the meal and balances heavier foods.
It is also easy to prepare at home using simple ingredients, and it can be adjusted based on personal preference. Some people add sweet corn, avocado, or even spaghetti to make it more unique.

Because of its rich ingredients and creamy dressing, it is both satisfying and enjoyable, making it a favorite for many people.
Whether served at a party or enjoyed at home, Ghanaian salad is a colorful, creamy, and delicious dish that brings freshness and flavor to every meal.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Lettuce 🥬
  2. Cabbage
  3. Tomatoes 🍅
  4. Cucumber 🥒
  5. Carrots 🥕
  6. Boiled eggs 🥚
  7. Baked beans
  8. Mayonnaise or salad cream

Optional:

  1. Sweet corn 🌽
  2. Pasta or potatoes 🥔
  3. Avocado 🥑

Steps to Make Ghanaian Salad👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Vegetables

  • Wash all vegetables
  • Chop lettuce, cabbage, tomatoes, cucumber, and carrots

2. Boil Eggs ⏱ (10–12 minutes)

  • Boil eggs until hard
  • Peel and slice

3. Prepare Other Ingredients ⏱ (5–10 minutes)

  • Open baked beans
  • Prepare any optional ingredients

4. Combine Ingredients

  • Add vegetables, eggs, and beans into a bowl

5. Add Dressing

  • Add mayonnaise or salad cream

6. Mix Gently

  • Stir carefully to combine

7. Chill (Optional) ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Refrigerate for better taste

8. Serve

  • Serve fresh or chilled
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh vegetables for best taste 🥗
  • Don’t add too much cream
  • Chill before serving for better flavor ❄️
  • Mix gently to keep texture
