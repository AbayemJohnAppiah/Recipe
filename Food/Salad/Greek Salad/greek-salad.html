Greek Salad

Greek Saladis a fresh and colorful dish that comes from Greece. It is known for its simple ingredients, natural flavors, and healthy benefits.
Greek salad is made mainly with fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and olives, combined with soft feta cheese and dressed with olive oil. Unlike many other salads, it does not rely on heavy creams or sauces, making it light, refreshing, and nutritious.
One of the best things about Greek salad is its freshness. The vegetables are usually raw and crisp, giving the salad a crunchy texture and natural sweetness. The feta cheese adds a slightly salty and creamy taste, while the olives bring a rich and tangy flavor.
The dressing is simple but very important. It is usually made with olive oil, sometimes with a little lemon juice or vinegar, which enhances the natural taste of the ingredients without overpowering them.
Greek salad is often served as a side dish, but it can also be eaten as a light meal, especially on hot days when you want something refreshing. It is very popular in restaurants and homes because it is easy to prepare and requires no cooking.

This salad is also considered healthy because it contains fresh vegetables, healthy fats from olive oil, and nutrients from cheese and olives.
Whether served as a side or main dish, Greek salad is a simple, fresh, and delicious meal that brings the taste of the Mediterranean to your table.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Tomatoes 🍅
  2. Cucumber 🥒
  3. Onion
  4. Olives
  5. Feta cheese 🧀
  6. Olive oil
  7. Salt
  8. Pepper
  9. Lemon juice (optional) 🍋

Steps to Make Greek Salad👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Vegetables

  • Wash tomatoes and cucumber
  • Cut into chunks
  • Slice onions

2. Combine Ingredients

  • Put vegetables in a bowl
  • Add olives

3. Add Cheese

  • Place feta cheese on top or mix lightly

4. Add Dressing

  • Pour olive oil
  • Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice

5. Mix Gently

  • Toss lightly to combine

6. Serve

  • Serve fresh
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh vegetables for best taste 🥗
  • Don’t overmix (keep texture)
  • Use good olive oil for better flavor
  • Serve immediately for freshness
