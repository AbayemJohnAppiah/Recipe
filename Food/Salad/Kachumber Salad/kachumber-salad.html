Kachumber Salad

Kachumber Salad is a simple and refreshing dish that comes from India. It is known for its fresh ingredients, crunchy texture, and light, natural flavor.
This salad is made using finely chopped vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions. These ingredients are mixed together to create a crisp and juicy texture that feels fresh in every bite.
One of the best things about Kachumber salad is its simplicity. It does not require cooking, and the ingredients are easy to find. The vegetables provide natural sweetness and freshness, while a simple dressing enhances the overall taste.
The dressing usually includes lemon juice, salt, and sometimes a little spice like chili or pepper. This adds a tangy and slightly spicy flavor, making the salad more exciting without making it heavy.
Kachumber salad is often served as a side dish with main meals such as rice, bread, or curry dishes. It helps balance heavier foods by adding freshness and crunch.
This salad is also very healthy because it contains fresh vegetables that are rich in vitamins and nutrients. It is light, refreshing, and perfect for hot weather or when you want something quick and healthy.

Because it is so easy to prepare, it is commonly made at home and served daily in many households.
Whether served alongside a meal or eaten on its own, Kachumber salad is a fresh, crunchy, and healthy dish that brings simplicity and flavor together.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Cucumber 🥒
  2. Tomatoes 🍅
  3. Onion
  4. Lemon juice 🍋
  5. Salt
  6. Pepper
  7. Chili (optional) 🌶️

Steps to Make Kachumber Salad👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Vegetables

  • Wash vegetables
  • Chop cucumber, tomatoes, and onions into small pieces

2. Combine Ingredients

  • Add chopped vegetables into a bowl

3. Add Dressing

  • Add lemon juice
  • Add salt, pepper, and chili

4. Mix Salad

  • Toss everything together

5. Serve

  • Serve fresh
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh vegetables for best taste 🥗
  • Chop evenly for better texture
  • Add chili for extra spice 🌶️
  • Serve immediately
