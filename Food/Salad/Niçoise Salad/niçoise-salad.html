Niçoise Salad

Niçoise Salad is a classic and elegant dish that comes from the city of Nice in France. It is known for its colorful presentation, fresh ingredients, and balanced flavors, making it one of the most famous salads in the world.
This salad is made using a combination of fresh vegetables, protein, and simple dressing. The main ingredients usually include tomatoes, green beans, boiled eggs, tuna, olives, and sometimes potatoes. Each ingredient is carefully arranged to create a visually appealing and well-balanced meal.
One of the unique things about Niçoise salad is that the ingredients are not heavily mixed. Instead, they are placed neatly in sections, allowing each flavor to stand out. The tuna adds a rich and savory taste, the eggs provide creaminess, and the vegetables bring freshness and crunch.
The dressing is usually simple, made with olive oil, sometimes with a touch of vinegar or lemon juice. This keeps the salad light while enhancing the natural flavors of the ingredients.
Niçoise salad is often served as a main dish because it is filling and nutritious. It provides a good balance of protein, vegetables, and healthy fats, making it both delicious and satisfying.

It is commonly enjoyed in restaurants and homes, especially during warm weather when a light but complete meal is preferred.
Whether served for lunch or dinner, Niçoise salad is a fresh, colorful, and well-balanced dish that represents simple yet refined cooking.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Tuna (canned or fresh) 🐟
  2. Eggs 🥚
  3. Tomatoes 🍅
  4. Green beans
  5. Potatoes 🥔
  6. Olives
  7. Olive oil
  8. Salt
  9. Pepper
  10. Lemon juice or vinegar 🍋

Steps to Make Niçoise Salad👩‍🍳

1. Boil the Eggs ⏱ (10–12 minutes)

  • Boil eggs until hard
  • Peel and cut into halves

2. Cook the Potatoes ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Boil potatoes until soft
  • Slice into pieces

3. Cook the Green Beans ⏱ (5–7 minutes)

  • Boil or steam green beans
  • Keep slightly firm

4. Prepare Vegetables

  • Wash and cut tomatoes

5. Arrange Ingredients

  • Place all ingredients on a plate
  • Arrange neatly (do not mix too much)

6. Add Tuna

  • Place tuna on top or in sections

7. Add Dressing

  • Drizzle olive oil
  • Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice

8. Serve

  • Serve fresh
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overcook vegetables
  • Arrange neatly for presentation 🎨
  • Use fresh ingredients for best taste
  • Keep dressing light
