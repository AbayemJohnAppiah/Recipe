Russian Salad

Russian Salad is a popular and satisfying salad known for its creamy texture and rich taste. It originated from Russia and has become widely enjoyed in many parts of the world.
This salad is made from a mix of cooked vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, and peas, combined with mayonnaise to create a smooth and creamy texture. Sometimes, ingredients like eggs, chicken, or apples are added to give it extra flavor and variety.
Russian salad is different from many fresh salads because the ingredients are usually boiled first. This makes the vegetables soft and easy to mix, giving the salad a thicker and more filling consistency.
One of the best things about Russian salad is its balance of flavors. The vegetables provide a mild and slightly sweet taste, while the mayonnaise adds creaminess and richness. When chilled, the flavors blend even better, making it very enjoyable to eat.
It is often served as a side dish during meals, celebrations, and special occasions. Because it is filling and creamy, it can also be eaten as a light meal on its own.

Russian salad is easy to prepare at home and does not require complicated ingredients. It is also a good way to use simple vegetables and turn them into a delicious dish.
Whether served cold as a side or enjoyed as a light meal, Russian salad is a creamy, comforting, and satisfying dish loved by many people around the world.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Potatoes 🥔
  2. Carrots 🥕
  3. Green peas
  4. Eggs 🥚
  5. Mayonnaise
  6. Salt
  7. Pepper

Optional:

  1. Cooked chicken 🍗
  2. Apples 🍎

Steps to Make Russian Salad👩‍🍳

1. Boil Vegetables ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Peel and cut potatoes and carrots
  • Boil until soft
  • Cook peas

2. Boil Eggs ⏱ (10–12 minutes)

  • Boil eggs
  • Peel and chop

3. Cool Ingredients ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Allow vegetables and eggs to cool

4. Combine Ingredients ⏱ (5–10 minutes)

  • Add vegetables and eggs to a bowl

5. Add Mayonnaise

  • Mix in mayonnaise
  • Add salt and pepper

6. Mix Well

  • Stir gently until evenly coated

7. Chill (Optional) ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Refrigerate for better taste

8. Serve

  • Serve cold
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overcook vegetables
  • Let ingredients cool before mixing
  • Chill for better flavor ❄️
  • Add mayonnaise gradually
