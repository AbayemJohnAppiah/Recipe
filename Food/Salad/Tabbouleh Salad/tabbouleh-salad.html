Tabbouleh Salad

Tabbouleh Salad is a fresh and vibrant salad that comes from the Middle Eastern region, especially Lebanon. It is known for its bright green color, refreshing taste, and light, healthy ingredients.
This salad is made mainly from finely chopped parsley, mixed with small amounts of bulgur wheat, tomatoes, onions, and fresh herbs. Unlike many salads, parsley is the main ingredient, giving the dish its unique flavor and appearance.
One of the most special things about tabbouleh is its freshness. The herbs are chopped finely, creating a soft yet slightly crunchy texture. The tomatoes add juiciness, while the bulgur provides a light and slightly chewy bite.
The dressing is simple but very important. It is made with olive oil and fresh lemon juice, which gives the salad a bright, tangy, and refreshing taste. This combination enhances the natural flavors without making the dish heavy.
Tabbouleh is often served as a side dish or appetizer, but it can also be enjoyed as a light meal. It is especially popular during warm weather because it is cool, refreshing, and not heavy on the stomach.
This salad is also considered very healthy because it is rich in fresh herbs, vitamins, and fiber. It is a great choice for people who want a nutritious and natural meal.
Whether served with other dishes or eaten on its own, tabbouleh salad is a fresh, light, and flavorful dish that brings a taste of healthy and simple cooking to your table.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Fresh parsley 🌿
  2. Bulgur wheat
  3. Tomatoes 🍅
  4. Onion
  5. Olive oil
  6. Lemon juice 🍋
  7. Salt
  8. Pepper

Steps to Make Tabbouleh Salad👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Bulgur

  • Soak bulgur in water
  • Let it soften

2. Chop Ingredients

  • Finely chop parsley
  • Cut tomatoes and onions

3. Drain Bulgur

  • Remove excess water

4. Combine Ingredients

  • Add bulgur, parsley, tomatoes, and onions to a bowl

5. Add Dressing

  • Pour olive oil and lemon juice
  • Add salt and pepper

6. Mix Well

  • Toss gently

7. Chill (Optional) ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Refrigerate for better flavor

8. Serve

  • Serve fresh or chilled
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Chop parsley very fine 🌿
  • Don’t use too much bulgur
  • Use fresh lemon for best taste 🍋
  • Chill before serving for freshness ❄️
