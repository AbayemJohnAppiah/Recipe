Oreo Ice Cream

Oreo Ice Cream is a rich, creamy, and delicious frozen dessert made by combining smooth vanilla ice cream with crushed Oreo cookies. It is loved for its perfect mix of creamy texture and crunchy cookie pieces, making every bite exciting and flavorful.
Oreo ice cream has a sweet vanilla base that blends perfectly with the chocolate cookie crunch from the Oreos. The cookies soften slightly in the ice cream, creating a “cookies and cream” texture that is both smooth and slightly crunchy at the same time.
One of the best things about Oreo ice cream is its balance of flavors. The sweetness of the cream pairs perfectly with the slightly bitter chocolate cookie, giving it a rich and satisfying taste.
The appearance is usually white or light cream with dark Oreo pieces mixed throughout, making it visually attractive and instantly recognizable.
Oreo ice cream is very versatile and can be enjoyed in many ways. It can be served in cones, bowls, or used in milkshakes, sundaes, and ice cream cakes.
It is especially popular among children and young people because of the familiar cookie taste and fun texture.

Making Oreo ice cream at home is simple and does not require many ingredients. It allows you to control the amount of cookies and creaminess to suit your taste.
Whether enjoyed as a simple dessert or a special treat, Oreo ice cream is a creamy, crunchy, and delightful dessert that delivers fun, sweetness, and satisfaction in every scoop.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Milk 🥛
  2. Heavy cream
  3. Sugar 🍯
  4. Vanilla extract 🌿
  5. Oreo biscuits 🍪

Steps to Make Oreo Ice Cream👩‍🍳

1. Crush Oreos

  • Break Oreo biscuits into small pieces

2. Mix Cream Base

  • Combine milk, cream, and sugar

3. Add Vanilla

  • Stir in vanilla extract

4. Heat Mixture

  • Warm gently (do not boil)

5. Cool Mixture

  • Let it cool completely

6. Add Oreos

  • Mix crushed Oreos into the base

7. Freeze ⏱ (3–5 hours)

  • Place in freezer until firm

8. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Scoop and enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t crush Oreos too fine for crunch 🍪
  • Add extra cookies for stronger flavor
  • Stir during freezing for smooth texture
  • Serve slightly soft for best taste 🍦
