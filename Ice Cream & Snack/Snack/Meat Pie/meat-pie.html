Meat Pie

Meat Pie is a delicious baked snack made from a soft, buttery pastry crust filled with seasoned minced meat, onions, carrots, and spices. It is one of the most popular snacks in many countries because of its rich flavor and satisfying filling.
Meat pie has a golden, flaky outer crust that is soft yet slightly crisp when baked properly. Inside, it contains a savory meat filling that is well-seasoned and flavorful, often mixed with vegetables to add texture and taste.
One of the best features of meat pie is its balance of texture and flavor. The soft pastry combined with the juicy meat filling creates a satisfying bite that is both comforting and filling.
Meat pies are commonly enjoyed as street food, school snacks, party treats, or quick meals. They are easy to carry and can be eaten anywhere, making them very convenient.
The appearance is usually golden brown and slightly glossy on top, often brushed with egg wash before baking to give it a beautiful finish. When cut open, the rich meat filling is visible inside the soft crust.
Meat pies are also very versatile. They can be made with beef, chicken, or even fish, and the seasoning can be adjusted to make them mild or spicy depending on preference.

Making meat pies at home allows you to control the ingredients and flavor, ensuring a fresh and delicious result every time.
Whether enjoyed warm or at room temperature, meat pie is a soft, savory, and satisfying snack that delivers rich flavor and comfort in every bite.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour 🌾
  2. Butter 🧈
  3. Egg 🥚
  4. Baking powder
  5. Salt 🧂
  6. Water 💧

For Filling:

  1. Minced meat 🍖
  2. Onion 🧅
  3. Carrot 🥕
  4. Salt & spices 🌶️
  5. Oil

Steps to Make Meat Pie👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Filling

  • Cook minced meat with onions and spices
  • Add chopped carrots
  • Let it cool

2. Make Dough

  • Mix flour, butter, egg, and water
  • Knead until smooth

3. Roll Dough

  • Roll dough flat on surface

4. Cut Shapes

  • Cut into circles

5. Fill Pies

  • Add meat filling inside dough
  • Fold and seal edges

6. Bake ⏱ (25–35 minutes)

  • Bake until golden brown

7. Cool & Serve

  • Let cool slightly and enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t overfill pies 🥧
  • Seal edges properly
  • Brush egg for shiny top
  • Bake until golden brown
