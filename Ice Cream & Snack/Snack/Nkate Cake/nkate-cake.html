Nkate Cake

Nkate Cake is a traditional Ghanaian sweet snack made from roasted peanuts and caramelized sugar. It is crunchy, sweet, and slightly nutty, making it a popular treat across Ghana and West Africa.
Nkate cake has a hard, crunchy texture and a rich peanut flavor. The roasted peanuts are coated in melted sugar that hardens when cooled, forming a brittle, candy-like snack that is both sweet and satisfying.
One of the most special things about nkate cake is its simplicity. It uses only a few ingredients, yet it delivers a strong, rich flavor that is loved by both children and adults.
It is commonly sold in markets, street stalls, and shops, often wrapped in small pieces of paper or plastic for easy carrying and eating.
The appearance is golden brown with visible peanut pieces inside the caramelized sugar. It is usually broken into flat or irregular shapes once cooled.
Nkate cake is also very filling and energy-rich because of the peanuts, making it a great quick snack during the day.
It is especially popular during festivals, travel, or as a simple treat at home. Its sweet and nutty flavor makes it addictive and enjoyable in small bites.

Making nkate cake at home is simple but requires careful heating of sugar to avoid burning. Once done properly, it creates a perfect crunchy sweet snack.
Whether enjoyed as a street snack or homemade treat, nkate cake is a crunchy, sweet, and nutty delight that delivers rich flavor and satisfying crunch in every bite.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Roasted peanuts 🥜
  2. Sugar 🍯
  3. Water 💧
  4. Salt (optional)

Steps to Make Nkate Cake👩‍🍳

1. Prepare Peanuts

  • Roast peanuts and remove skin

2. Melt Sugar

  • Heat sugar with a little water until caramel forms

3. Add Peanuts

  • Mix peanuts into melted sugar

4. Shape Quickly ⏱ (5 minutes)

  • Pour mixture onto flat surface
  • Spread evenly before it hardens

5. Cool

  • Let it harden completely

6. Cut or Break

  • Break into pieces

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Enjoy crunchy snack

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t burn sugar 🍯
  • Work fast before it hardens
  • Use roasted peanuts for best flavor 🥜
  • Store in dry container for crunch
