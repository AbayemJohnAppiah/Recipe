Pretzels

Pretzels are a popular baked snack made from dough that is shaped into a distinctive knot or twist, then baked until golden brown. They are known for their slightly chewy texture, shiny brown crust, and often salted surface.
Pretzels have a unique flavor that is slightly salty with a soft, doughy inside and a firm outer crust. The combination of soft and chewy texture makes them very enjoyable to eat.
One of the most recognizable features of pretzels is their twisted knot shape. This shape is not only traditional but also helps give them their signature texture when baked.
Pretzels are very versatile and can be enjoyed in both sweet and savory forms. Some are sprinkled with coarse salt, while others are coated with cinnamon sugar or served with cheese dips or chocolate.
They are popular in many countries, especially as street food, bakery snacks, or movie treats. Pretzels are often enjoyed warm and fresh from the oven for the best taste.
The appearance is shiny brown on the outside with a soft interior. The salt crystals on top add both flavor and texture.

Making pretzels at home is a fun process that involves shaping dough, boiling it briefly, and then baking it to achieve the perfect texture and color.
Whether enjoyed as a snack, party food, or bakery treat, pretzels are a chewy, salty, and satisfying snack that delivers flavor, texture, and comfort in every bite.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour 🌾
  2. Yeast
  3. Sugar 🍯
  4. Salt
  5. Warm water 💧
  6. Butter 🧈
  7. Baking soda (for boiling)
  8. Coarse salt (optional)

Steps to Make Pretzels👩‍🍳

1. Mix Dough

  • Combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt, water, and butter
  • Knead until smooth

2. Let Dough Rise ⏱ (1 hour)

  • Cover and allow dough to rise

3. Shape Pretzels

  • Roll dough into ropes
  • Twist into pretzel shape

4. Boil Pretzels

  • Dip in baking soda water briefly

5. Bake ⏱ (12–20 minutes)

  • Bake until golden brown

6. Add Salt

  • Sprinkle coarse salt on top

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Enjoy warm

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t skip boiling step 🥨
  • Shape evenly for proper baking
  • Serve warm for best taste
  • Try sweet version with cinnamon sugar 🍯
