Puff Puff

Puff Puff is a soft, fluffy, and slightly sweet fried dough snack made from flour, sugar, yeast, and water. It is one of the most popular street snacks in Ghana, Nigeria, and many other African countries.
Puff puff has a light and airy texture on the inside with a slightly crispy golden-brown outside. When freshly fried, it is soft, warm, and melts slightly in your mouth, making it very enjoyable and addictive.
One of the most special things about puff puff is its simplicity. It is made from basic ingredients, but the result is a delicious snack that is loved by both children and adults.
Puff puff is often served at parties, gatherings, street food stalls, and celebrations. It is commonly eaten as a snack on its own or paired with drinks like juice or tea.
The appearance is round, golden brown dough balls that look slightly puffy and soft. Some versions are dusted with sugar or flavored with spices like nutmeg for extra taste.
Puff puff can also be customized. Some people add milk, eggs, or even fillings like chocolate or coconut to make it richer and more flavorful.

Making puff puff at home is simple but requires time for the dough to rise properly. Once fried, it becomes soft, fluffy, and delicious.
Whether enjoyed fresh from the oil or cooled slightly, puff puff is a soft, fluffy, and sweet snack that delivers warmth, comfort, and delicious taste in every bite.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Flour 🌾
  2. Sugar 🍯
  3. Yeast
  4. Warm water 💧
  5. Salt
  6. Nutmeg or vanilla 🌿
  7. Oil for frying

Steps to Make Puff Puff👩‍🍳

1. Mix Dry Ingredients

  • Combine flour, sugar, yeast, salt, and nutmeg

2. Add Water

  • Add warm water and mix into smooth batter

3. Let Dough Rise ⏱ (1–2 hours)

  • Cover and allow mixture to rise until fluffy

4. Heat Oil

  • Heat oil in deep pan

5. Fry Puff Puff

  • Scoop batter into oil
  • Fry until golden brown

6. Drain Oil

  • Place on paper towel

7. Serve ⏱ (Immediately)

  • Enjoy warm

Tipes 💡

  • Let dough rise well for softness ⏱️
  • Don’t overcrowd oil while frying 🔥
  • Use medium heat for even cooking
  • Serve warm for best taste 🍩
