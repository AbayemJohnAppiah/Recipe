Beans & Plantain

Beans & Plantain, also known as Gobe, is one of Ghana's most loved traditional meals. It is commonly sold by street food vendors and is enjoyed in homes, schools, and workplaces because it is both delicious and satisfying.😝 The dish is made by slowly cooking beans until they become soft and tender. The beans are then mixed with a flavorful palm oil sauce made from onions, pepper, and sometimes tomatoes. This gives it a rich and spicy taste that makes it very enjoyable.😜🙃 The meal is served with freshly fried ripe plantains, which add a sweet balance to the spicy beans. Some people also add extras like boiled eggs, gari, or spaghetti to make it more filling. Gobe is not just food-it is a comfort meal in Ghana. It is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner and is known for keeping people full for a long time. Whether bought from a street vendor or cooked at home, it always delivers a rich and satisfying taste.😘

A warm plate of Beans & Plantain brings the true taste of Ghana to your table-soft beans, sweet plantain, and spicy palm oil sauce coming together in perfect harmony.😍😋

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Beans (Black-Eyed Peas)
  2. Ripe Plantain
  3. Palm Oil
  4. Onion
  5. Pepper
  6. Tomatoes (optional)
  7. Salt & Seasoning
  8. Gari (optional)
  9. Boiled Eggs (optional)

Steps to Make Beans & Plantain (Gobe) 👩‍🍳

1. Cook the Beans ⏱ (45–60 minutes)

  • Wash the beans well
  • Put in a pot with water
  • Cook until soft (about 45-60 minutes)

2. Prepare the Stew ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Heat palm oil in a pan
  • Add chopped onions
  • Add pepper and tomatoes
  • Fry for a few minutes
  • Add salt and seasoning

3. Mix Beans with Stew (5–10 minutes)

  • Add cooked beans into the stew
  • Stir well
  • Let it cook together for 5-10 minutes

4. Fry the Plantain

  • Peel ripe plantain
  • Slice into pieces
  • Fry in hot oil until golden brown

5. Serve

  • Put beans on a plate
  • Add fried plantain
  • Add gari or egg if you like

Tipes 💡

  • Use ripe plantain for sweet taste
  • Don’t overcook beans(it becomes too soft)
  • Add a little ginger for extra flavor
  • Serve hot for best taste 🔥
