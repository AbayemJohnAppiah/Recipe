Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a colorful, flavorful, and widely loved dish enjoyed in Ghana and around the world. It is made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, spices, and protein such as chicken, shrimp, or eggs. The result is a delicious meal that is both satisfying and visually appealing.
Fried rice is known for its bright mix of ingredients like carrots, green peas, sweet corn, and spring onions. These vegetables not only add color but also give the dish a fresh and crunchy texture. The rice is usually cooked first and then fried with oil, soy sauce, and spices to give it a rich taste and aroma.
In Ghana, fried rice is especially popular at parties, restaurants, and special occasions. It is often served with fried chicken, salad, or shito, making it a complete and enjoyable meal. Many people love it because it is tasty, easy to prepare, and can be customized with different ingredients.
One of the best things about fried rice is its flexibility. You can use leftover rice, add your favorite vegetables, and choose any protein you like. This makes it a perfect meal for both beginners and experienced cooks.

Fried rice is also a quick meal option when you want something delicious without spending too much time cooking. The combination of flavors, textures, and colors makes it very appealing and enjoyable for all ages.
Whether served at a party or prepared at home, fried rice remains a classic dish that brings together taste, simplicity, and creativity in one plate.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Cooked rice
  2. Vegetable oil
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic (optional)
  5. Mixed vegetables
    (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  6. Eggs
  7. Chicken or shrimp (optional)
  8. Soy sauce & Spring onions
  9. Salt & Seasoning cubes

Steps to Make Fried Rice👩‍🍳

1. Cook the Rice ⏱ (20–25 minutes)

  • Wash the rice
  • Cook until soft but
    not too mushy
  • Allow it to cool

2. Prepare Ingredients

  • Chop vegetables
  • Beat eggs
  • Cut chicken or shrimp

3. Cook the Protein ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Fry chicken or shrimp
    until cooked
  • Set aside

4. Fry the Eggs ⏱ (3–5 minutes)

  • Pour beaten eggs into
    a pan
  • Scramble lightly
  • Set aside

5. Stir-Fry Vegetables ⏱ (5–10 minutes)

  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Add onions and garlic
  • Add vegetables and
    stir-fry

6. Add Rice

  • Add cooked rice to the pan
  • Mix well with vegetables

7. Add Sauce & Seasoning

  • Add soy sauce, salt,
    and seasoning
  • Stir properly

8. Combine Everything

  • Add cooked chicken
    and eggs
  • Mix well

9. Final Cooking

  • Stir and allow everything
    to blend
  • Add spring onions

10. Serve

  • Serve hot
  • Enjoy with chicken
    or salad

Tipes 💡

  • Use cold rice
    (not freshly hot rice)
  • Don’t overcook vegetables
    (keep them crunchy)
  • Add soy sauce gradually
  • Stir continuously to avoid
    burning 🔥
