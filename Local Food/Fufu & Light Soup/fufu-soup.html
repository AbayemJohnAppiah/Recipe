Fufu & Light Soup

Fufu & Light Soup is one of the most traditional and beloved meals in Ghana. It is a rich, comforting dish that combines soft, smooth fufu with a light, spicy, and flavorful soup.
Fufu is made by boiling cassava and plantain (or yam) and pounding them together until they become soft, stretchy, and smooth. It has a mild taste, which makes it perfect for absorbing the rich flavor of the soup. The texture of fufu is very important-it should be soft and easy to swallow.
Light soup is a thin but very flavorful soup made from fresh tomatoes, pepper, onions, and spices. It is usually cooked with meat, fish, or chicken, which gives it a deep and rich taste. Despite being called “light,” the soup is full of strong flavors and can be quite spicy depending on how it is prepared.
This dish is widely eaten across Ghana and is often served during family gatherings, special occasions, and even everyday meals. It is known for its comforting and satisfying nature, making it a favorite for many people.
Eating Fufu & Light Soup is also a cultural experience. Traditionally, the fufu is eaten with the hands by pinching a small portion, dipping it into the soup, and swallowing without chewing too much. This unique way of eating makes the meal even more enjoyable and meaningful.
Fufu & Light Soup is not just a meal—it is a symbol of Ghanaian culture, tradition, and togetherness. Whether served with goat meat, fish, or chicken, it remains one of the most iconic and cherished dishes in Ghanaian cuisine.

Ingredients 🥕

For Fufu:

  1. Cassava
  2. Plantain (or yam)
  3. Water

For Light Soup:

  1. Fresh tomatoes
  2. Pepper
  3. Onion
  4. Meat, fish, or chicken
  5. Salt
  6. Seasoning cubes
  7. Garlic & ginger (optional)

Steps to Make Fufu & Light Soup👩‍🍳

How to prepare Fufu:

1. Boil Cassava & Plantain ⏱ (20–25 minutes)

  • Peel and wash
  • Boil until soft

2.Pound the Fufu

  • Pound in a mortar
  • Mix until smooth and stretchy

3. Shape the Fufu

  • Mold into round balls

How to prepare Light Soup:

1. Cook the Meat ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Season meat with salt, onion, garlic, and ginger
  • Cook until tender

2. Prepare the Base

  • Blend or grind tomatoes, pepper, and onion
  • Add to the meat

3. Cook the Soup ⏱ (20–25 minutes)

  • Add water to desired level
  • Allow it to boil well
  • Let flavors mix

4. Add Fish or Extra Protein

  • Add fish or chicken
  • Cook until done

5. Final Taste ⏱ (5 minutes)

  • Add salt and seasoning
  • Adjust taste

6. Serve

  • Serve fufu with hot light soup
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Pound fufu well for smooth texture
  • Don’t make soup too thick
  • Use fresh ingredients for best taste 🍅
  • Serve hot for maximum enjoyment 🔥
