Jollof Rice & Chicken

Jollof Rice & Chicken is one of the most popular and loved dishes across West Africa, especially in Ghana and Nigeria. It is a rich, flavorful one-pot rice dish cooked in a spicy tomato sauce and served with well-seasoned fried or grilled chicken.
The dish is made by cooking rice in a mixture of blended tomatoes, pepper, onions, and spices. This allows the rice to absorb all the flavors, giving it a deep red color and a smoky, delicious taste. The chicken is usually marinated with spices, then fried or grilled to give it a crispy outside and juicy inside, making it a perfect combination with the rice.
Jollof Rice is not just food-it is a cultural symbol. It is served at weddings, parties, family gatherings, and special occasions. The smell alone can bring people together, and it is often the center of celebration meals.

There is also a famous and ongoing comparison between Ghana Jollof and Nigeria Jollof, often called the “Jollof rivalry.”
👉 Ghana Jollof is known for its rich, smoky flavor and deep seasoning. It is often cooked slowly to allow the rice to absorb the sauce fully, giving it a slightly soft but flavorful texture. Many people say Ghana Jollof has a stronger taste because of the spices and cooking method.
👉 Nigeria Jollof, on the other hand, is usually lighter in texture and sometimes less oily. It often has a brighter red color and a slightly different spice balance. Nigerian Jollof is also very delicious and is widely loved, especially for its party-style cooking.

Both versions are amazing, and the choice between them often depends on personal taste. However, one thing is certain-Jollof Rice & Chicken remains one of the most iconic and enjoyable meals in Africa.

Ingredients 🥕

  1. Rice
  2. Chicken
  3. Tomatoes (fresh or paste)
  4. Onion
  5. Pepper
  6. Vegetable oil
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Salt
  9. Garlic & ginger
  10. Curry powder & thyme

Steps to Make Jollof Rice & Chicken 👩‍🍳

1. Season the Chicken

  • Wash the chicken
  • Add salt, seasoning,
    garlic, ginger,and spices
  • Mix well and allow it to
    marinate

2. Cook the Chicken ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Fry or grill the chicken until
    golden brown
  • Set aside

3. Prepare the Jollof Base ⏱ (15 minutes)

  • Blend tomatoes, pepper,
    and onion
  • Heat oil in a pot
  • Fry the blended mixture
  • Add seasoning, curry,
    and thyme
  • Cook until thick

4. Add Rice

  • Wash the rice properly
  • Add it to the sauce
  • Stir well so it mixes evenly

5. Cook the Rice ⏱ (20–30 minutes)

  • Add water or chicken stock
  • Cover and cook on low heat
  • Stir occasionally to
    prevent burning

6. Final Steam & Serve

  • Allow the rice to steam
    well
  • Serve with the fried or
    grilled chicken

Tipes 💡

  • Use chicken stock instead of
    water for better taste
  • Don’t add too much water
    (it can spoil the texture)
  • Let it cook slowly for a
    smoky flavor 🔥
  • Stir gently to avoid
    breaking the rice
