Braised Rice & Pepper

Braised Rice & Green Pepper Sauce is a simple yet bold and flavorful dish enjoyed across Ghana. It combines soft, well-cooked rice prepared with vegetable oil and served with a fresh, spicy green pepper sauce that brings out a strong and exciting taste.
Braised rice is made by cooking rice in vegetable oil with onions, salt, and sometimes light seasoning. The vegetable oil gives the rice its beautiful orange color and a rich traditional flavor that is both satisfying and comforting. It is a quick and easy alternative to more complex rice dishes, making it very popular in many Ghanaian homes.
The green pepper sauce is what makes this dish truly special. It is prepared using fresh green pepper, onions, and sometimes tomatoes, blended or roughly ground to keep a slightly coarse texture. The sauce is often mixed with fried fish, boiled eggs, or meat, giving it extra flavor and making the meal more filling. Its spicy and slightly smoky taste creates a perfect contrast with the mild, Braised rice.
This dish is commonly eaten for lunch or dinner and is loved for its simplicity and strong flavor. It is also a go-to meal when you want something quick but still very delicious. Many people enjoy it with fried fish or eggs, which adds both protein and texture to the meal.
Braised Rice & Green Pepper Sauce is also a comfort food that reminds many people of home cooking. It shows how simple ingredients like rice, oil, and pepper can be turned into a rich and satisfying meal.

Whether served in a home kitchen or enjoyed at a local food spot, this dish represents the beauty of Ghanaian cooking—simple, bold, and full of flavor.

Ingredients 🥕

For Braised Rice:

  1. Rice
  2. Vegetable oil
  3. Onion
  4. Salt
  5. Water

For Pepper Sauce:

  1. Fresh pepper(Red/Green)
  2. Fresh tomatoes
  3. Onion
  4. Salt

Steps to Make Braised Rice & Pepper👩‍🍳

How to prepare Braised Rice:

1. Wash the Rice

  • Wash rice thoroughly
  • Remove dirt and excess
    starch

2. Prepare the Base ⏱ (3–6 minutes)

  • Heat palm oil in a pot
  • Add chopped onions
  • Fry lightly

3. Add Rice

  • Add washed rice
  • Stir well so oil coats
    the rice

4. Cook the Rice ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Add water and a little salt
  • Cover and cook until soft

5. Steam the Rice

  • Allow rice to steam well
  • Fluff gently

How to prepare Pepper Sauce:

1. Wash clean ingredients

  • Fresh pepper(Red or Green)
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onion

2. Ingredients into a
Blender or Asanka

  • Put all ingredients into
    a Blender or an Asanka
    and blend
  • Add salt taste

3. Serve

  • Serve Braised Rice with
    Pepper Sauce
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Don’t add too much
    vegetable oil
  • Use fresh pepper for
    strong taste 🌶️
  • Stir rice gently to
    avoid breaking it
  • Serve hot for best
    flavor 🔥
