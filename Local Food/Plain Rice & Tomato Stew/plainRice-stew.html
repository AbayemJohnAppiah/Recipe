Plain Rice & Tomato Stew

Plain Rice & Tomato Stew is a simple yet very popular meal enjoyed in Ghana and many parts of West Africa. It is a combination of soft, fluffy white rice served with a rich, flavorful tomato stew made with fresh ingredients and spices.
Plain rice is prepared by boiling rice in water until it becomes soft and fluffy. It has a mild taste, which makes it a perfect base for different types of stews and sauces. Because it is simple, it allows the flavor of the stew to stand out.
Tomato stew is the highlight of this dish. It is made by blending fresh tomatoes, onions, and pepper, then cooking them slowly in oil with spices and seasoning. The stew can be prepared with different proteins such as fish, chicken, eggs, or meat, making it very flexible and suitable for different tastes. Its rich red color, slightly spicy flavor, and oily texture make it very appetizing.
This meal is commonly eaten at home and is also served in restaurants and food joints because it is easy to prepare and very satisfying. It can be enjoyed at any time of the day-breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Plain Rice & Tomato Stew is also a comfort meal for many people. It is affordable, quick to prepare, and can be served with sides like fried plantain, salad, or boiled eggs. Despite its simplicity, it delivers a delicious and fulfilling experience that keeps people coming back to it.
Whether served with crispy fried fish, juicy chicken, or just eggs, Plain Rice & Tomato Stew remains one of the most loved everyday meals in Ghanaian homes.

Ingredients 🥕

For Plain Rice:

  1. Rice
  2. Water
  3. Salt

For Tomato Stew:

  1. Fresh tomatoes or
    tomato paste
  2. Onion
  3. Pepper
  4. Vegetable oil
  5. Fish, chicken, eggs,
    or meat
  6. Salt
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Garlic & ginger
    (optional)

Steps to Make Plain Rice & Tomato Stew👩‍🍳

How to prepare Plain Rice:

1. Wash the Rice

  • Wash rice properly to
    remove dirt and starch

2. Boil the Rice ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Add water and a little salt
  • Cook until soft

3. Drain and Steam

  • Drain excess water
    (if needed)
  • Cover and allow it
    to steam

How to prepare Tomato Stew:

1. Prepare Ingredients

  • Blend tomatoes, onions,
    and pepper

2. Fry the Base ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Heat oil
  • Fry onions and blended
    mixture
  • Cook until thick

3. Add Protein ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Add fish, chicken, or meat
  • Allow it to cook well

4. Season the Stew ⏱ (5 minutes)

  • Add salt and seasoning
    cubes
  • Stir well

5. Final Cooking

  • Allow stew to cook until
    oil rises to the top

6. Serve

  • Serve rice with tomato
    stew
  • Add plantain, egg, or
    salad if you like

Tipes 💡

  • Use fresh tomatoes for
    better taste 🍅
  • Don’t add too much water
    to rice
  • Fry stew well to remove
    sour taste
  • Add fried plantain for
    extra flavor 🍌
