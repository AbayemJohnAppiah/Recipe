Rice Balls & Groundnut Soup

Rice Balls & Groundnut Soup is a traditional and highly satisfying meal enjoyed across Ghana. It combines soft, smooth rice balls with a rich, creamy, and flavorful soup made from groundnuts (peanuts), creating a perfect balance of texture and taste.
Rice balls, locally known as omo tuo, are made by cooking rice with extra water and mashing it until it becomes soft and smooth. The rice is then shaped into round balls, making it easy to dip into the soup. Its mild taste allows it to absorb the rich flavor of the soup, making every bite delicious.
Groundnut soup is one of the most popular soups in Ghana. It is prepared using groundnuts blended into a smooth paste and cooked with tomatoes, onions, pepper, and spices. The soup is often enriched with meat, chicken, fish, or even goat, giving it a deep and savory flavor. Its creamy texture and slightly nutty taste make it very unique and enjoyable.
This dish is commonly served at home, during special occasions, and even at local restaurants. It is known for being very filling and nourishing, providing both energy and important nutrients.
Eating Rice Balls & Groundnut Soup is also a cultural experience. Traditionally, the rice balls are gently broken with the fingers and dipped into the soup, allowing the rich flavors to soak in before eating. The combination of the soft rice and the warm, flavorful soup makes it a true comfort meal.

Whether enjoyed on a quiet day at home or served to guests, Rice Balls & Groundnut Soup represents warmth, tradition, and the rich taste of Ghanaian cuisine.

Ingredients 🥕

For Rice Balls:

  1. Rice
  2. Water
  3. Salt

For Groundnut Soup:

  1. Groundnuts (peanut paste)
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Onion
  4. Pepper
  5. Meat, chicken, or fish
  6. Salt
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Garlic & ginger (optional)

Steps to Make Rice Balls & Groundnut Soup👩‍🍳

How to prepare Rice Balls (Omo Tuo):

1. Wash the Rice

  • Wash the rice properly
  • Remove dirt and excess starch

2. Cook the Rice ⏱ (20–25 minutes)

  • Add enough water
    (more than normal rice)
  • Cook until very soft

3. Mash the Rice

  • Use a wooden spoon
    to mash the rice
  • Stir until smooth and sticky

4. Shape into Balls

  • Scoop and mold into
    round balls
  • Set aside

How to prepare Groundnut Soup:

1. Cook the Meat ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Season meat with salt,
    onion, and spices
  • Cook until tender

2. Prepare the Base ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Blend tomatoes, pepper,
    and onion
  • Add to the meat
  • Cook well

3. Add Groundnut Paste

  • Mix peanut paste with
    water
  • Add to the pot
  • Stir well

4. Cook the Soup

  • Allow soup to cook until
    thick
  • Stir occasionally to avoid
    burning

5. Final Taste & Serve

  • Add salt and seasoning
  • Serve hot with rice balls

Tipes 💡

  • Use smooth peanut paste
    for better texture
  • Stir well to avoid lumps
    in the soup
  • Don’t make the soup too thick
  • Add smoked fish for richer
    flavor 🐟
