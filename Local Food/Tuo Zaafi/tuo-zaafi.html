Tuo Zaafi

Tuo Zaafi (often called TZ) is a traditional and nutritious meal from the northern regions of Ghana. It is made from maize flour cooked into a soft, smooth, and slightly stretchy dough, usually served with a light soup or green vegetable soup.
Tuo Zaafi is known for its simple ingredients but rich cultural value. The maize flour is carefully cooked and stirred until it becomes smooth and lump-free, creating a soft texture that is easy to eat and very satisfying. It has a mild taste, which allows it to blend perfectly with flavorful soups.
This dish is commonly served with soups made from green leaves, such as ayoyo or spinach, often cooked with meat, fish, or local spices. The soup is usually light, slightly slimy, and very nutritious, making it a perfect match for the soft TZ.
Tuo Zaafi is widely eaten in Northern Ghana and is an important part of the local culture and daily meals. It is often prepared at home and served during family gatherings and special occasions.
One of the unique things about TZ is how it is eaten. Like many traditional dishes, it is usually enjoyed with the hands. Small portions are dipped into the soup and swallowed, creating a smooth and enjoyable eating experience.

This dish is also valued for its health benefits. It is light on the stomach, easy to digest, and provides good energy.
Whether served with ayoyo soup or another local soup, Tuo Zaafi represents tradition, simplicity, and the rich food culture of Northern Ghana.

Ingredients 🥕

For Tuo Zaafi:

  1. Maize flour
  2. Water

For Soup:

  1. Ayoyo leaves or spinach
  2. Meat or fish
  3. Onion
  4. Pepper
  5. Salt
  6. Seasoning

Steps to Make Tuo Zaafi (TZ)👩‍🍳

1. Boil Water

  • Pour water into a pot
  • Bring to a boil

2. Prepare Flour Mix

  • Mix maize flour with a little water
  • Stir to form a smooth paste

3. Add to Boiling Water

  • Pour the paste into the boiling water
  • Stir continuously

4. Cook and Stir ⏱ (15–20 minutes)

  • Add more flour gradually
  • Stir strongly to avoid lumps
  • Cook until thick and smooth

5. Shape the TZ

  • Scoop and mold into round portions

6. Serve

  • Serve hot with soup
  • Enjoy

Tipes 💡

  • Stir continuously to avoid lumps
  • Don’t make it too hard or too soft
  • Use enough water for smooth texture
  • Serve hot for best taste 🔥
