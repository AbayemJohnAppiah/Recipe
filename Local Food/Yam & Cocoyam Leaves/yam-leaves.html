Yam & Cocoyam Leaves

Yam & Cocoyam Leaves Stew (often called “kontomire stew”) is a deeply traditional and nutritious dish enjoyed across Ghana. It combines soft boiled yam with a rich green stew made from cocoyam leaves, creating a balanced meal that is both filling and full of flavor.
Yam is a popular staple food in Ghana, known for its smooth texture and ability to keep people full for a long time. When boiled, it becomes soft and slightly firm, making it perfect for pairing with soups and stews. Its mild taste allows it to blend well with strongly flavored sauces.
The cocoyam leaves stew is the highlight of this dish. Fresh cocoyam leaves are carefully washed, blended, and cooked with ingredients like palm oil, onions, pepper, tomatoes, and protein such as fish, meat, or eggs. The leaves give the stew a rich green color and a unique earthy flavor that makes it very special.
This dish is not only delicious but also very healthy. Cocoyam leaves are rich in vitamins and nutrients, while yam provides energy. Together, they create a meal that is both nourishing and satisfying.

Yam and Cocoyam Leaves Stew is commonly prepared in homes and served during family meals. It is also enjoyed during special occasions and is loved for its comforting taste and cultural importance. Whether eaten alone or shared with others, it represents the warmth and richness of Ghanaian cuisine.

Ingredients 🥕

For Yam:

  1. Yam
  2. Water
  3. Salt

For Cocoyam Leaves Stew:

  1. Cocoyam leaves (kontomire)
  2. Palm oil
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Onion
  5. Pepper
  6. Fish, meat, or eggs
  7. Salt
  8. Seasoning cubes

Steps to Make Yam & Cocoyam Leaves Stew👩‍🍳

How to prepare Yam:

1. Peel and Cut Yam

  • Peel the yam
  • Cut into medium pieces
  • Wash thoroughly

2. Boil the Yam ⏱ (20–25 minutes)

  • Put yam in a pot with water
  • Add a little salt
  • Cook until soft

How to prepare Cocoyam Leaves Stew:

1. Prepare the Leaves ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Wash the cocoyam
    leaves well
  • Blend or chop them finely

2. Cook the Base ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Heat palm oil in a pot
  • Add chopped onions,
    pepper, and tomatoes
  • Fry until well cooked

3. Add Protein ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Add fish, meat, or eggs
  • Allow to cook until tender

4. Add Cocoyam Leaves ⏱ (10–15 minutes)

  • Add the blended leaves
  • Stir well and let it cook
  • Add salt and seasoning

5. Final Cooking

  • Allow everything to
    cook together
  • Stir gently

6. Serve

  • Serve hot with boiled yam
  • Enjoy the rich taste

Tipes 💡

  • Do not overcook the
    leaves (to keep nutrients)
  • Use fresh leaves for
    best taste
  • Add smoked fish for
    extra flavor 🐟
  • Make sure yam is soft
    but not too watery
